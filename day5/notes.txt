_________***RANGE()***_________
Range functions returns a sequence of numbers, stsrting from 0 by 
default, and increments by 1(by default), and stops before a specified number.

range(start?,stop,step?)
for el in range(5):
    print(el)
for el in range(1,5):
    print(el)
for el in range(1,5,2):
    print(el)
